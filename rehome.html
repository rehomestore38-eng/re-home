The Process

How RE HOME Works

Three simple steps — connecting your old items to someone who needs them, fully repaired.

01
You Sell Your Item
List your used electronics, furniture, or appliances. Our team visits, inspects, and offers a fair price. Quick and hassle-free pickup.
02
We Repair & Upgrade
Our certified technicians clean, repair, and upgrade items to near-new condition using quality components and professional care.
03
We Resell Like New
Refurbished items are listed with a quality certificate at 40–70% off retail price — giving budget buyers access to great products.

Our Mission

Saving Money,
Saving the Planet

RE HOME was founded with one belief: that well-made products deserve a second chance. We connect conscious buyers and sellers, building a community that values sustainability over disposal.

Reduce Electronic Waste

India generates over 3 million tonnes of e-waste annually. We extend product lifespans to fight that number every single day.

Empower Budget Buyers

Not everyone can afford brand-new. We make quality accessible, letting families and students get great products for less.

Fair Value for Sellers

Instead of throwing away or getting cheated, sellers receive fair prices and know their items go to good homes.
